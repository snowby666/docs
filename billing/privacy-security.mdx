## <Icon icon="eye-off" /> No-Log Policy for Prompts

<Card title="We do not log your prompt content" icon="shield" color="#10B981">
  Your prompts and AI responses are not stored in our logs. We only log:
  - <Icon icon="info" /> Request metadata (model used, tokens, credits, timestamp, status)
  - <Icon icon="server" /> Technical information (IP address, API key used, route accessed)
  - <Icon icon="bar-chart" /> Usage statistics (for billing and analytics)
</Card>

<Card title="What this means" icon="check-circle" color="#8B5CF6">
  - <Icon icon="lock" /> Your conversations and prompts remain private
  - <Icon icon="eye-off" /> We cannot see what you're asking the AI
  - <Icon icon="file-x" /> We cannot reconstruct your conversations from our logs
  - <Icon icon="shield-check" /> Your data privacy is protected
</Card>

## <Icon icon="filter" /> Content Moderation

To comply with our AI provider content policies and legal requirements, we apply moderation to detect and flag requests involving:

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="CSAM Detection" icon="alert-triangle" color="#EF4444">
    **CSAM (Child Sexual Abuse Material)**: Any content related to child exploitation
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Illegal Activities" icon="ban" color="#F59E0B">
    Content about making drugs, bombs, weapons, or other illegal activities
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Harmful Content" icon="shield-alert" color="#EF4444">
    Content that could cause real-world harm
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Card title="How it works" icon="settings" color="#8B5CF6">
  - <Icon icon="scan" /> Requests are automatically scanned before being sent to AI providers
  - <Icon icon="block" /> Flagged requests are blocked and not processed
  - <Icon icon="coins" /> No credits are charged for blocked requests
  - <Icon icon="check" /> This helps us maintain compliance with provider policies and legal requirements
</Card>

<Card title="Your privacy is still protected" icon="shield-check" color="#10B981">
  - <Icon icon="bot" /> Moderation checks are automated and do not involve human review of your content
  - <Icon icon="trash" /> We do not store the content of blocked requests
  - <Icon icon="info" /> This is a necessary measure to ensure service availability and legal compliance
</Card>
