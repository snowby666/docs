# <Icon icon="wallet" /> Pay-as-You-Go (Permanent Credits)

Pay-as-You-Go allows you to purchase permanent credits that never expire. This gives you maximum flexibility without any recurring commitment.

<Callout type="info">
  <Icon icon="infinity" /> Permanent credits <strong>never expire</strong> - they stay in your account forever!
</Callout>

## <Icon icon="coins" /> What are Permanent Credits?

<Card title="Permanent Credits" icon="wallet" color="#10B981">
  **Permanent Credits** are credits you purchase with real money. Unlike daily credits, these credits:
  - <Icon icon="infinity" /> **Never expire** - they stay in your account forever
  - <Icon icon="credit-card" /> Can be purchased through PayPal
  - <Icon icon="layers" /> Can be used alongside daily credits
  - <Icon icon="trending-up" /> Have volume discounts (the more you spend, the more bonus credits you get)
</Card>

## <Icon icon="dollar-sign" /> Pricing

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Base Rate" icon="calculator" color="#8B5CF6">
    **`$1` USD = 3 credits**
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Volume Discounts" icon="percent" color="#10B981">
    - Every `$500` spent: **10% bonus**
    - Maximum discount: **30% bonus** (at `$1,500+` total spending)
  </Card>
</Columns>

<Card title="Pricing Examples" icon="chart-line" color="#F59E0B">
  - First purchase: `$100` = 300 credits (no bonus yet)
  - After `$500` total: `$100` purchase = 330 credits (10% bonus)
  - After `$1,500` total: `$100` purchase = 390 credits (30% bonus, maximum)
</Card>

## <Icon icon="shopping-cart" /> How to Purchase

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard">
    Go to the [Electron Hub Dashboard](https://playground.electronhub.ai/console)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Payment">
    Navigate to the credits/payment section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Amount">
    Select the amount you want to purchase
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Complete the payment through PayPal
  </Step>
  <Step title="Credits Added">
    Credits are added to your account immediately after payment <Icon icon="check-circle" />
  </Step>
</Steps>

## <Icon icon="shield" /> Payment Limits

<Card title="Purchase Limits" icon="lock" color="#EF4444">
  To prevent abuse, there are limits on purchases:
  - <Icon icon="calendar" /> **Daily limit**: 20 orders per day
  - <Icon icon="calendar-days" /> **Monthly limit**: `$5,000` USD per month
  - <Icon icon="refresh-cw" /> These limits reset automatically
</Card>

## <Icon icon="gauge" /> Monthly Spending Limits

<Card title="Spending Controls" icon="sliders" color="#8B5CF6">
  You can set a **monthly spending limit** for permanent credits to control costs:
  - **0 = Unlimited** (no limit)
  - **Any positive number** = Maximum amount you can spend per month
  - **Maximum limit**: `$100,000` USD per month
  - Limits reset on the 1st of each month
</Card>

<Callout type="tip">
  <strong>Use cases:</strong>
  - Budget control for teams
  - Preventing accidental overspending
  - Managing costs for production applications
</Callout>

## <Icon icon="gauge" /> Rate Limit Scaling

<Card title="Automatic Scaling" icon="trending-up" color="#10B981">
  Your rate limits scale based on your **total purchased credits** (permanent credits spending). 
  
  See [Rate Limit Scaling](/billing/advanced#rate-limit-scaling) for details.
</Card>

## <Icon icon="bell" /> Low Balance Alerts

<Card title="Stay Informed" icon="mail" color="#F59E0B">
  You can enable **low balance alerts** for permanent credits:
  - Set a threshold (e.g., 10 credits)
  - Receive email alerts when your permanent credits balance drops below the threshold
  - Alerts are sent at most once every 24 hours (to prevent spam)
</Card>

## <Icon icon="undo" /> Refund Policy

<Callout type="info">
  <strong>Permanent Credits Purchases:</strong> Refunds are available within 30 days of purchase if you haven't used the credits. Once credits have been used, refunds are not available except in cases of technical errors or service issues.
</Callout>
