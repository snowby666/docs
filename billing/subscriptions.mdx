Subscriptions provide weekly credits that automatically refill every Sunday, along with access to premium features and models.

| Tier | Weekly Credits | Max Bots | Studio Credits |
|------|----------------|----------|----------------|

| Plus | $20 | 30 | 50 |
| Core | $40 | 50 | 75 |
| Pro | $60 | 100 | 150 |
| Business | $120 | 150 | 300 |
| Enterprise | $250+ (custom) | 200 | 500 |

<Card title="Benefits of Higher Tiers" icon="arrow-up" color="#8B5CF6">

## Enterprise Tier (Custom Plan)

The **Enterprise tier** is a custom plan that scales with your Ko-fi payment amount:

<Card title="Base Benefits" icon="gift" color="#F59E0B">
  - Base weekly credits: **\$250**
  - Base monthly cost: `$249`
  - 200 max bots
  - 500 studio credits
</Card>

<Card title="Custom Scaling" icon="arrow-up" color="#8B5CF6">
  If you pay more than the base cost on Ko-fi, your weekly credits scale automatically:
  
  **Extra credits formula**: For every dollar above the base cost, you get **1 additional credit per week**
  
  **Example**: If you pay `$300`/month instead of `$249`:
  - Extra paid: `$300` - `$249` = `$51`
  - Extra credits: `$51` Ã— 1 = \$51 credits
  - **Total weekly credits**: \$250 + \$51 = **\$301/week**
</Card>

<Info>
  **How it works:**
  - Your Ko-fi payment amount is automatically detected
  - Weekly credits are calculated based on your actual payment
  - Credits update automatically when your payment changes
  - Works for both monthly and yearly subscriptions (yearly payments are divided by 12 for monthly calculation, with a 20% annual discount)
</Info>

<Tip>
  This allows Enterprise users to customize their plan based on their needs and budget.
</Tip>

## How to Subscribe

Monthly subscriptions are managed through **Ko-fi memberships**. To subscribe:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your link code">
    Go to the [Electron Hub Dashboard](https://app.electronhub.ai#console) â†’ **Billing** section and generate your Ko-fi link code (it looks like `KF-XXXXXXXX`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Subscribe on Ko-fi">
    Visit the Electron Hub Ko-fi page and join the monthly membership for your desired tier.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your link code">
    Paste your link code (`KF-XXXXXXXX`) into the **message field** on Ko-fi so we can credit the payment to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate Benefits">
    Once Ko-fi confirms the payment (usually within ~1 minute), your subscription benefits are applied automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  <strong>Important:</strong> Always include your link code (`KF-XXXXXXXX`) in the Ko-fi message field. If it's missing, we fall back to matching by the **verified email** on your Ko-fi payment, but the link code is the most reliable way to credit your account.
</Warning>

## Subscription Renewal

<Card title="Automatic Renewal" icon="repeat" color="#8B5CF6">
  Monthly subscriptions renew automatically through your Ko-fi membership cycle. Your subscription expiry date is shown in your dashboard.
</Card>

## What Happens When Subscription Expires?

<Warning>
  If your subscription expires:
  - You'll be downgraded to the Free tier
  - You'll receive \$0.25 weekly credits
  - You'll lose access to premium/ultimate models
  - Your permanent credits remain (they never expire)
  - Your flex credits remain (if you have any)
</Warning>

## Weekly Credits Refill

<Info>
  Weekly credits refill every **Sunday at 21:00 UTC** (9:00 PM UTC). Unused weekly credits do <strong>not</strong> carry over to the next week - this is why [Flex Credits](/billing/credits#flex-credits) are useful for saving unused credits.
</Info>
