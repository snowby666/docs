Subscriptions provide weekly credits that automatically refill every Sunday, along with access to premium features and models.

## Subscription Tiers

Electron Hub offers several subscription tiers through Patreon:

| Tier | Weekly Credits | Max Bots | Studio Credits |
|------|----------------|----------|----------------|
| Free | $0.25 | 5 | 5 |
| Starter | $8 | 20 | 20 |
| Plus | $15 | 30 | 50 |
| Core | $30 | 50 | 75 |
| Pro | $50 | 100 | 150 |
| Business | $100 | 150 | 300 |
| Enterprise | $200+ (custom) | 200 | 500 |

<Card title="Benefits of Higher Tiers" icon="arrow-up" color="#8B5CF6">
  - More weekly credits
  - Access to premium/ultimate models
  - Higher rate limits
  - More bot creation slots
  - More studio credits (for AI bot creation)
</Card>

## Annual vs Monthly Billing

<Card title="Save 20% with Annual Billing" icon="percent" color="#10B981">
  **Annual subscriptions save you 20%** compared to monthly billing!
  
  **Example:**
  - Monthly Pro tier: `$69`/month
  - Annual Pro tier: `$69` × 12 × 0.8 = `$662.40`/year (Save 20%)
</Card>

<Tip>
  Annual subscriptions are perfect for users who:
  - Plan to use the service long-term
  - Want to maximize value
  - Prefer fewer payment transactions
</Tip>

## Enterprise Tier (Custom Plan)

The **Enterprise tier** is a custom plan that scales with your Patreon payment amount:

<Card title="Base Benefits" icon="gift" color="#F59E0B">
  - Base weekly credits: **$200**
  - Base monthly cost: `$249`
  - 200 max bots
  - 500 studio credits
</Card>

<Card title="Custom Scaling" icon="arrow-up" color="#8B5CF6">
  If you pay more than the base cost on Patreon, your weekly credits scale automatically:
  
  **Extra credits formula**: For every dollar above the base cost, you get **1 additional credit per week**
  
  **Example**: If you pay `$300`/month instead of `$249`:
  - Extra paid: `$300` - `$249` = `$51`
  - Extra credits: `$51` × 1 = $51 credits
  - **Total weekly credits**: $200 + $51 = **$251/week**
</Card>

<Info>
  **How it works:**
  - Your Patreon payment amount is automatically detected
  - Weekly credits are calculated based on your actual payment
  - Credits update automatically when your payment changes
  - Works for both monthly and yearly subscriptions (yearly payments are divided by 12 for monthly calculation, with a 20% annual discount)
</Info>

<Tip>
  This allows Enterprise users to customize their plan based on their needs and budget.
</Tip>

## How to Subscribe

Subscriptions are managed through **Patreon**. To subscribe:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Purchase on Patreon">
    Visit the Electron Hub Patreon page and select your desired tier.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access Dashboard">
    Go to the [Electron Hub Dashboard](https://playground.electronhub.ai/console) → **Billing** section
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link Accounts">
    Link your Patreon account to your Electron Hub account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate Benefits">
    Your subscription benefits will be applied automatically
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  <strong>Important:</strong> You must purchase the subscription on Patreon first before linking your account. If you link your account without an active Patreon subscription, you won't receive any benefits.
</Warning>

## Subscription Renewal

<Card title="Automatic Renewal" icon="repeat" color="#8B5CF6">
  Subscriptions renew based on your Patreon subscription cycle (usually monthly or annually). Your subscription expiry date is shown in your dashboard.
</Card>

## What Happens When Subscription Expires?

<Warning>
  If your subscription expires:
  - You'll be downgraded to the Free tier
  - You'll receive $0.25 weekly credits
  - You'll lose access to premium/ultimate models
  - Your permanent credits remain (they never expire)
  - Your flex credits remain (if you have any)
</Warning>

## Weekly Credits Refill

<Info>
  Weekly credits refill every **Sunday at 21:00 UTC** (9:00 PM UTC). Unused weekly credits do <strong>not</strong> carry over to the next week - this is why [Flex Credits](/billing/credits#flex-credits) are useful for saving unused credits.
</Info>
