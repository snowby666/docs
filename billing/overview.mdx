Electron Hub offers two flexible billing models to suit different usage patterns and needs:

## Billing Models

### 1. Subscriptions (Recurring)

**Subscriptions** provide you with daily credits that reset every day at 21:00 UTC. This model is ideal for:
- Regular, consistent usage
- Predictable monthly costs
- Access to premium/ultimate models
- Higher rate limits and more features

**Benefits:**
- Daily credits automatically added to your account
- Access to premium models (Pro tier and above)
- Higher rate limits
- More bot creation slots
- More studio credits

**Annual Savings:**
- **Annual subscriptions save you 20%** compared to monthly billing
- Perfect for long-term users who want to maximize value

### 2. Pay-as-You-Go (Permanent Credits)

**Pay-as-You-Go** allows you to purchase permanent credits that never expire. This model is ideal for:
- Irregular or unpredictable usage
- One-time projects
- Testing and experimentation
- Supplementing subscription credits

**Benefits:**
- Credits never expire
- Volume discounts available (up to 30% bonus)
- No recurring commitment
- Can be used alongside subscription credits
- Rate limit scaling based on total purchases

## Which Model Should I Choose?

**Choose Subscriptions if:**
- You use the service regularly
- You want predictable monthly costs
- You need access to premium models
- You prefer automatic daily credits

**Choose Pay-as-You-Go if:**
- Your usage is irregular or unpredictable
- You're testing the service
- You want to supplement your subscription credits
- You prefer one-time purchases

**You can use both!** Many users combine subscriptions with permanent credits for maximum flexibility.
